The Life of Sushant

1986: A star is born.

As a child, Sushant was always interested in acting and was very fascinated by theatre and film. Rajput was reportedly an avid reader who was deeply interested in astrophysics and won the National Olympiad in Physics.

Soon afterward, he also began attending acting classes under the theatre director Barry John. Rajput later stated that he found the experience to be liberating and wanted to continue doing it forever.

Rajput was subsequently called for an audition and cast as a second lead in the television series Kis Desh Mein Hai Meraa Dil in 2008.

On 25 September 2014, Sushant Singh Rajput was announced as the actor who would play the role of Dhoni in the biopic which was titled M.S. Dhoni: The Untold Story and directed by Neeraj Pandey.
